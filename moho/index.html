Who are we?

Welcome to our home! Moishe House Somerville is the Inman Square home of Alex, Akiva, Ben & Shira, where we host cocktail parties, arts & crafts nights, shabbat dinners & more in Inman Square.

We're here to create a strong, fun and caring Jewish community, through food, learning, cocktails and music. Anyone, regardless of background is invited to all our events! If you have questions or need our address, message us on Facebook.

All our events show up here at the start of the month, but you can stay updated by joining our newsletter or subscribing to our gcal.
